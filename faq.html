Frequently asked question

What is UX design?

User Experience (UX) design is the process of designing products and services that are easy, enjoyable, and relevant for users.

What are the key principle ofUX design?

The key principles of UX design include prioritizing user needs, ensuring usability, maintaining consistency, utilizing visual hierarchy, empowering user control, and embracing accessibility.

What is the difference between ui and ux design?

UI (User Interface) design focuses on the visual aesthetics and interactivity of a product, while UX (User Experience) design focuses on the overall user journey and how they feel while interacting with it.

What is the wireframe?

A wireframe is a simplified, basic layout that outlines the structure of a website, app, or other digital product.
